Our Mission

Clharity empowers the charitable mindset by creating a revolutionary environment between donors and charities. We focus on providing our valued donors with authenticated transactions that enable our users to scour the globe for their most impactful causes. Our platform embellishes organizations that display a noble cause, allowing charities to be evaluated on their goals and responsibilities rather than their popularity.





Our Process



Process for Donors - Step 1


The experience of Clharity begins once a donor has identified the cause they wish to support. Donors can then browse through charities across the world to find the perfect charity for their interests. Once a charity has been selected, donors can rest assured their contribution will be utilized in an efficient way that reflects the intention of the transaction.

Process for Donors - Step 2


Donors are notified through a comprehensive report about the details of their contribution as soon as their donation has been spent. This report will detail the use of their contribution in the development of the charity and in the organization's pursuit of its goal. Once the report is complete, we at Clharity hope that donors and charities can maintain the relationship they have established through communication and updates on the progress of the mission.

Process for Charities


The first step is reaching an agreement with Clharity about the best-intended purpose of the contributions they recieve. After the details have been established, we begin to ensure their transparency as they list on our site as an official charity. By listing on our platform, charities gain access to our diverse donor base, while being presented our one-of-a-kind benefits to help establish and grow support for the organization's cause. We ensure our best possible customer service for all charities, catering to any need or request that a charity might have in a timely manner.
