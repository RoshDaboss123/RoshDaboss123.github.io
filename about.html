How Clharity Works

Managing donation payments have never been easier, through Clharity's revolutionary donating process.

  • Contact + Research Charity

    Contact us, and we'll research numerous nonprofits regarding your desired cause using our tested AI model, and recommend the most efficient, trustworthy, and impactful to you.

  • Make a Donation

    Donate a select amount through Clharity to a nonprofit of your choosing. There will be no transaction fee, meaning that 100% of your donation goes to the charity.

  • Charity Spends Donation

    The charity recieves your donation, and spends it to further their cause.

  • Donor Report

    Charity sends back a holistic impact report detailing how the donation was spent.
