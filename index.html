Our mission

Clharity empowers the charitable mindset by creating a revolutionary environment between donors and charities. We focus on providing our valued donors with authenticated transactions that enable our users to scour the globe for their most impactful causes.


Our platform embellishes organizations that display a noble cause, allowing charities to be evaluated on their goals and responsibilities rather than their popularity.





Donors

Donors are provided with a comprehensive report detailing the authenticated effects of their contribution as soon as their donation has been spent.


We at Clharity hope that donors and charities can maintain the relationship they have established through communication and updates on the progress of the mission.





Charities

Charities reach an agreement about the best intended contributions that they recieve. Once the details have been established, our exclusive benefits are presented.


Listing on our platform empowers charities with access to a global donor base, enhanced visbility to targeted donors, and numerous other services to help your charity grow, all at zero cost.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


Why Donate Through Clharity?

Clharity not only has a minimal transaction rate that beats out almost all other fundraising services, but we also provide a personal level connection between the charity and you by providing a filtered report about exactly where your donation is going and to what purpose!

Why is there a transaction fee?

The Transaction Fee is used to sustain the company, providing sufficient maintenance funds to properly authenticate the transactions of your favorite charities.

How can my charity join Clharity?

We welcome all official charities to join our family here at Clharity! To join us, simply press this link, or email customerrelations@clharity.com to get started. After the request is processed, we will reach out to you to set up a meeting where we will briefly explain the process.
