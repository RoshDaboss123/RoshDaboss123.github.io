Who We Are

Clharity's Mission

Clharity's mission is to validate and secure the ethical use of contributions, providing vital support to growing charitable organizations.

We guarantee donations fulfill their intended purpose while upholding transparency and impact, empowering donors to make a meaningful difference.

Our Story

Every Donation Tells a Story

Clharity was founded to target the concept of donor skepticism, or when donors doubt the impact of impact or legitimacy of a nonprofit's cause, thus limiting the amount of donations they recieve.
Clharity uses a revolutionary process to remove this issue in the donation process, ensuring total confidence to the donor that their donation is being spent charitably by the nonprofit.
