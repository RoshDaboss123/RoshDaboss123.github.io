Our Story

Clharity started as an idea to a business pitch competition. We pitched our idea to a team of industry professionals, winning first place in the competition. From then on, we knew that our value proposition was unique, and that we were tackling an issue that was previously unaddressed.


As a result, we became determined to turn our business pitch idea into an actual company, aimed at alleviating donor skepticism in the charity world. As with all things, our initial startup wasn't easy. We lacked clients, and funding, and connections. We were forced to cold-email charities, pitching them our idea. We met with industry professionals, seeking feedback on our value proposition.


Eventually, we began to recieve emails back from potential clients; some were positive, others simply waived off our idea, as they'd never heard of us. Desperate to build our name in the crowdfunding industry, we began developing our website, and simultaneously writing blogs to help improving our search engine optimization. In time, our name shot up to the top of search engine search results, allowing us to begin formally partnering with charities like Moving America Forward, Kids Against Hunger, and Pivotal.

We had also developed a model that was trained on millions of IRS 990 forms, that cohesively produced an accurate, holistic rating of any charity. This algorithm, we believe, was really our main differentiator that allowed our business to stand out.

Today, Clharity has facilitated over $30,000 in donations between high-level donors and well-known charities. Yet our journey has just begun; we hope to be one of the biggest-names in the crowdfunding industry in the future.
