Clharity's Services

Making well-informed decisions about your charitable donations is crucial. At Clharity, we offer a range of services to simplify this process for donors.

Donation Reporting

Clharity offers detailed donation reports that clearly outline how contributed funds are allocated by the nonprofit. This ensures donors have precise information about the utilization of their donations.

Financial Examinations

Clharity examines the financial records, historical impacts, and current operations of all recommended NPOs to ensure transparency and accountability.

Published AI Model

Our AI model, developed using millions of nonprofit tax reports, assesses organizations based on their impact per dollar donated. This guarantees our recommendations pinpoint the most effective nonprofits for donors to support within a particular cause.

Nonprofit Researching:

Clharity enables donors to specify a particular cause, and we leverage extensive databases to identify the most efficient nonprofits within that cause. We then provide the donor with a list of 3-5 recommended nonprofits for their selection.

    FAQ

    Below you'll find answers to Clharity's most asked questions.

    Why donate through Clharity?

    Clharity is FREE! We take no transaction fee and authenticate your donation for the sole purpose of helping alleviate any suspicion of intent you may have regarding the spending of your contribution. All donations using Clharity are completely tax deductible and our comprehensive impact report provides proprietary statistics to better evaluate growth and efficiency in nonprofits.

    Why is Clharity a For-Profit Company?

    At Clharity, we believe that the more you give, the more you get, but for us to start giving, we need investments! A nonprofit business structure doesn’t attract investors because it doesn’t provide any potential for a return on investment. Although Clharity is currently a C-corporation, because of our socially and environmentally beneficial goals, we hope to attain B-corporation certification.

    How does Clharity evaluate nonprofits?

    Clharity utilizes our published algorithm along with individual nonprofit comparisons through evaluations of IRS 990 form data, public customer reviews, and third-party evidence on nonprofit impact. Once a donation has been transacted, we work directly with the nonprofit recipient to ascertain the exact usage of your funds. From the data collected from each of these methods, Clharity is able to formulate an accurate reflection of the nonprofit’s efficiency and predicted growth.
