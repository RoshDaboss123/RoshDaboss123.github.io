Benefits

Learn about the coveted benefits Clharity provides for its partners.

Donor Benefits



Filtered Report

Each time a charity spends a donation, the donor receives a comprehensive report on the usage of that money and how it has affected lives.

View Report

Automatic Authentication

Donors can search for the most meaningful causes, and we employ a fool-proof system that ensures the efficiency of charity growth and spending, without the need for a thrid party to provide us our data.


Refined Metrics

We provide donors with comprehensive metrics, which include numerous facets of charity expenditures, that reduce the time required to investigate charities. Our metrics are designed to holistically evaluate charities, giving donors a true impression of the likely result of their donation.



Charity Benefits



Credibility

A charity's source of revenue fundamentally comes down to their ability to retain credibility. By providing a filtered report to all donors, they can rest assured knowing where their hard-earned contributions are going, ultimately creating a personal level of trust between the donor and the charity. This opens up the door to a system of future recurring donations.


Personalize Customer Experience

Donors are given options to donate on a recurring basis by accepting packages distributed by the charity. This typically looks like a donor accepting a monthly recurring donation in exchange for a steady stream of media on the development of the charity.


Platform

Our charity dashboard allows charities to view their donor analytics. These metrics will likely differ from previously analyzed donor bases, as we provide a donor base diversified across the world, and empowered to donate to charities thousands of miles away. We hope that charities are able to mitigate marketing costs with an extremely effective marketing strategy.


Certification Stamp

Fixed fee paid to monitor payments indefinitely and guarantee their credibility.

Charity can place the stamp anywhere on its website so that donors can rest assured their money is being spent properly, and they will get the same benefits as donating to a charity on our website. Charity can use the stamp to justify its authentication and garner more donations.
